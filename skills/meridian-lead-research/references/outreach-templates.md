# Outreach Templates

Messaging frameworks and templates for L&D buyer outreach.

---

## Core Value Proposition

**One-liner**: AI-powered career mentor that helps employees upskill for the AI era

**Elevator Pitch**:
> Skillia is a personalized learning companion that analyzes each employee's skills, identifies gaps, and creates tailored development paths. We help companies prepare their workforce for AI disruption—not with generic courses, but with AI-guided career mentorship that adapts to each person.

**Pilot Pitch**:
> We start with a 3-month paid pilot (€3-5K for 50-100 users) so you can validate ROI before company-wide rollout. Most pilots convert to full deployment within 60 days.

## Industry-Specific Messaging

### Tech/Software

**Pain Point**: AI is changing faster than your team can upskill

**Message Framework**:
> "[Company] is clearly investing in AI—we saw [signal: job posting/announcement]. The challenge most tech teams face is keeping skills current when the landscape shifts quarterly.
>
> Skillia provides each developer an AI mentor that analyzes their current skills, maps them to your tech roadmap, and creates personalized learning paths. No more generic courses that don't stick.
>
> Would a 15-minute demo be worth it to see how we're helping [similar company] reskill 400 engineers?"

**Conversation Starters**:
- "How are you handling the AI skills gap across your engineering team?"
- "What's your biggest challenge with developer retention and growth?"
- "Are your L&D programs keeping pace with your tech stack evolution?"

---

### Healthcare/Biotech

**Pain Point**: Compliance training is broken, and digital transformation isn't optional

**Message Framework**:
> "Healthcare organizations like [Company] are under pressure from two directions: compliance requirements that demand verified training, and digital transformation that requires entirely new skill sets.
>
> Skillia helps by personalizing learning for each employee—clinical staff get pathways tailored to their role, while ensuring compliance training is tracked and verified.
>
> We're working with several healthcare organizations on pilots. Would it be worth 15 minutes to show you the compliance tracking dashboard?"

**Conversation Starters**:
- "How are you managing compliance training across different clinical roles?"
- "What's the biggest friction point in your digital health transformation?"
- "How personalized is your current learning for different staff types?"

---

### Manufacturing/Industry 4.0

**Pain Point**: Your workforce is anxious about automation, and traditional training isn't helping

**Message Framework**:
> "The shift to Industry 4.0 at [Company] is exciting—but we're hearing from manufacturers that their biggest challenge isn't the technology, it's preparing the workforce.
>
> Skillia helps by giving each employee a personalized reskilling path. Instead of generic 'digital skills' courses, we analyze what they know, what their new role requires, and build a bridge.
>
> Would it be helpful to see how we're supporting [similar manufacturer] through their automation transition?"

**Conversation Starters**:
- "How is your team responding to the Industry 4.0 skill requirements?"
- "What's your approach to transitioning existing employees vs hiring new?"
- "Are you seeing resistance or anxiety around automation from the floor?"

---

### Professional Services

**Pain Point**: Training competes with billable hours, and AI tools are changing everything

**Message Framework**:
> "At [Company], I imagine training time competes directly with client work. But with AI tools reshaping [consulting/legal/accounting], the firms that invest in continuous learning will win.
>
> Skillia solves this by making learning continuous and efficient—personalized paths that fit into workflow, not day-long workshops that kill utilization.
>
> We're piloting with several [law firms/consulting firms]. Would 15 minutes be worth it to compare approaches?"

**Conversation Starters**:
- "How do you balance professional development with billable targets?"
- "What's your firm's approach to AI tool adoption across teams?"
- "How personalized are your associate development programs?"

## Pilot Program Pitch

**Standard Pilot Terms**:
- Duration: 3 months
- Users: 50-100 employees
- Investment: €3,000-5,000
- Credits toward annual contract if converted

**Pilot Value Props**:
1. **Low risk** - Validate ROI before full commitment
2. **Fast setup** - Live within 2 weeks
3. **Clear metrics** - Dashboard shows engagement, skill development
4. **Conversion credit** - Pilot cost applies to annual contract

**Pilot Close Script**:
> "Most organizations start with a 3-month pilot—it lets you see real results with your actual employees before any larger commitment. The pilot runs €3-5K depending on user count, and that investment credits toward annual if you convert. Should we scope a pilot for [department/team]?"

## LinkedIn Outreach Templates

### Connection Request
> Hi [Name], I noticed [Company] is [specific observation—hiring, expanding, transformation]. We're helping similar [industry] companies prepare their workforce for AI—would be great to connect.

### First Message (After Connection)
> Thanks for connecting, [Name]. I saw [Company] recently [signal]. We're working with [industry] companies on personalized employee upskilling—curious if that's on your radar? Happy to share what's working for others.

### Follow-up (No Response)
> Hi [Name], following up on my earlier note. Quick question: how is [Company] approaching AI skills development? We're seeing interesting patterns across [industry] and thought you might have perspective.

## Objection Responses

### "We already have LinkedIn Learning/Coursera"
> "Those are great content libraries. What we hear is they're not personalized enough—completion rates drop because the content isn't relevant to each person's actual role. Skillia is the guidance layer that tells each employee what to learn and why."

### "Budget is locked for this year"
> "Totally understand. Our pilot model is specifically designed for this—€3-5K lets you validate ROI this year, and if it works, you'll have data to justify budget for next year. Would a Q1 pilot make sense?"

### "We're in the middle of another transformation"
> "That's exactly when this is most valuable. Transformation fails when people don't have the skills to execute. We can align the learning paths to support your transformation goals specifically."

### "We need to see ROI first"
> "Absolutely. That's why we start with pilots. We'll set up a dashboard that tracks engagement, skill progression, and time-to-competency. You'll have hard data within 90 days."

|-------|--------|---------|---------|
