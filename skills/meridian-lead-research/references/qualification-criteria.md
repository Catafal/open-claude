# Lead Qualification Criteria

Scoring rubric and prioritization matrix for Skillia leads.

---

## Scoring Formula

**Total Score = (ICP Fit × 0.4) + (Buying Signals × 0.3) + (Accessibility × 0.2) + (Timing × 0.1)**

Each component scored 1-10, final score normalized to 10.

---

## ICP Fit Scoring (40% weight)

### Company Size Score
| Size | Score |
|------|-------|
| 500-1,500 | 10 |
| 300-500 | 8 |
| 1,500-2,000 | 8 |
| 200-300 | 5 |
| <200 or >2,500 | 2 |

### Industry Score
| Industry | Score |
|----------|-------|
| Tech/Software | 10 |
| Healthcare/Biotech | 10 |
| Manufacturing/Industry 4.0 | 10 |
| Professional Services | 9 |
| Financial Services | 7 |
| Retail/Other | 5 |

### Geographic Score
| Location | Score |
|----------|-------|
| Catalonia | 10 |
| Spain (other) | 8 |
| Portugal | 7 |
| EU (France, Germany) | 6 |
| Other EU | 5 |

**ICP Fit = (Size + Industry + Geography) / 3**

---

## Buying Signals Score (30% weight)

### Strong Signals (+3 each, max 10)
- Active L&D job posting (last 30 days)
- Digital transformation announcement (last 90 days)
- Recent funding round (last 6 months)
- AI adoption initiative announced
- Workforce expansion >20% planned

### Moderate Signals (+2 each)
- L&D content shared by executives on LinkedIn
- Training partner relationship mentioned
- New facility/office opening
- Industry award for people/culture

### Weak Signals (+1 each)
- General "great place to work" positioning
- Presence at HR industry events
- Employee testimonials about development

### Negative Signals (-2 each)
- Recent layoffs announced
- Cost-cutting initiatives mentioned
- Just purchased competitor LMS
- Leadership turnover in HR/L&D

## Lead Tier Classification

| Tier | Total Score | Action |
|------|-------------|--------|
| A (Hot) | 8.0-10.0 | Immediate outreach, personalized |
| B (Warm) | 6.0-7.9 | Prioritize for outreach, standard |
| C (Nurture) | 4.0-5.9 | Add to pipeline, monitor for signals |
| D (Archive) | <4.0 | Document and archive, revisit later |

## Quick Qualification Checklist

Before adding to output, verify:

- [ ] Company size 300-2,000 employees
- [ ] Industry matches target (Tech/Healthcare/Manufacturing/Services)
- [ ] Located in Spain or target EU market
- [ ] No recent competitor purchase visible
- [ ] Decision maker identifiable
- [ ] At least one buying signal present
- [ ] Total score ≥ 5.0 for inclusion
